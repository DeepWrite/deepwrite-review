---
title: "Evidence And Citation Policy"
subtitle: "How claims become publishable."
permalink: /evidence-policy/
---

Every contested factual claim must be traceable to a source. Every statistical claim must include source, date, unit, geography, denominator, known limitations, and the basis for comparison.

Evidence levels are `high`, `moderate`, `contested`, `low`, and `discourse_only`.

If a claim cannot be verified, it must be marked as unverified or removed.
