---
title: "Editorial Policy"
subtitle: "How the magazine decides what matters."
permalink: /editorial-policy/
---

Each issue asks what changed this quarter, what matters structurally, which institutions or incentives are driving change, which interpretations compete, what evidence is strong or weak, and what readers should watch next.

Every major article must distinguish fact, interpretation, hypothesis, normative judgment, and speculation. It must include a best opposing view and an evidence strength section.

The magazine may take positions, but only after reconstructing the strongest rival positions.
