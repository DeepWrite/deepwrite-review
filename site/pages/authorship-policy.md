DeepWrite Review does not use fictional personal bylines or real public figures as placeholder authors.

The default public byline is:

```text
DeepWrite Review Editorial Desk
Prepared with Codex editorial agents.
Reviewed and approved for publication by Jeyoun Son (손제연).
```

한국어판의 기본 표기는 다음과 같다.

```text
DeepWrite Review 편집실
Codex 편집 에이전트의 도움을 받아 작성되었습니다.
손제연 책임편집자의 검토와 발행 승인을 거칩니다.
```

Codex may assist with research, drafting, review, translation, and production. Final publication remains subject to Chief Editor approval.
