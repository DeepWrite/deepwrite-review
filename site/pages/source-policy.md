---
title: "Source Policy"
subtitle: "A hierarchy for evidence and argument."
permalink: /source-policy/
---

Tier 1 sources include official statistics, court decisions, legislation, government reports, international organizations, peer-reviewed literature, and established datasets.

Tier 2 sources include university research centers, major polling organizations, transparent think tank reports, and major newspapers or magazines with strong editorial standards.

Tier 3 sources include expert commentary, interviews, newsletters, and opinion essays.

Tier 4 sources include social media, partisan blogs, viral posts, and unsourced claims. Tier 4 sources may be used only as evidence of discourse, not as evidence of fact.
