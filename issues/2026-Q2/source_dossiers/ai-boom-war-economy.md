# Source Dossier: The AI Boom Meets The War Economy

## Core Question

How should readers understand AI investment when it is occurring inside a macro environment shaped by war risk, energy pressure, trade friction, and industrial policy?

## Provisional Thesis

AI is becoming a general-purpose infrastructure before the world has restored geopolitical and macroeconomic stability; this makes the AI boom less a clean productivity story than a stress test of energy systems, capital allocation, labor adjustment, and state strategy.

## Strongest Opposing Thesis

AI investment may be the stabilizing force of the quarter: a productivity-enhancing capital cycle that supports growth despite geopolitical disruption.

## Evidentiary Gaps

- Causal attribution between AI investment and macro resilience is likely weak.
- Layoff claims attributed to AI require careful denominator and alternative-cause checks.

## Risks Of Overstatement

- Treating all AI capital expenditure as productive investment.
- Treating all AI-related layoffs as caused by AI.
- Confusing chip-cycle strength with broad economic transformation.
