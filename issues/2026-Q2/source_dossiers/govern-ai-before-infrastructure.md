# Source Dossier: Can The World Govern AI Before It Becomes Infrastructure?

## Independent Reporting Assignment

This article is assigned to the Technology Editor with Law and Institutions Editor. It must proceed as a magazine article, not a compact briefing skeleton. The public article should carry argument, evidence, cases, and prose; development notes belong here or in review files.

## Central Thesis

AI governance is moving from principles to implementation while AI becomes operating infrastructure; the decisive issue is whether public institutions can classify, inspect, contest, update, and legitimate systems before dependency hardens.

## Korean Thesis Check

AI가 작동 인프라가 되는 동안 AI 거버넌스는 원칙에서 실행으로 이동하고 있다. 결정적 쟁점은 의존이 굳어지기 전에 공적 제도가 시스템을 분류하고, 점검하고, 다투고, 갱신하고, 정당화할 수 있는가이다.
