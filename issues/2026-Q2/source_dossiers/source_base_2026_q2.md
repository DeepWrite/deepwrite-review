## Korean Commentary Firewall

Korean-language columns, opinion essays, critical essays, and already-interpreted newspaper or magazine articles may be used only for:

- discourse evidence
- media-attention evidence
- agenda-setting evidence
- evidence that a controversy or interpretive frame exists

They may not be used as raw evidence for:

- factual claims
- statistical claims
- legal claims
- institutional descriptions
- causal inference
- policy-effect claims

## Dossier Opening Rule

Each article dossier should begin with raw or primary sources, then add academic literature, then high-quality journalism for chronology and context, and only then add commentary as discourse evidence.
