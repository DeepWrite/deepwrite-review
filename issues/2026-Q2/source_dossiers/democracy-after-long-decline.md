# Source Dossier: Democracy After The Long Decline

## Independent Reporting Assignment

This article is assigned to the Political Editor with Law and Institutions Editor. It must proceed as a magazine article, not a compact briefing skeleton. The public article should carry argument, evidence, cases, and prose; development notes belong here or in review files.

## Central Thesis

Democratic decline in 2026 is best read as a normalization problem: erosion becomes dangerous when the institutions of correction still exist formally but lose practical authority.
