# Source Dossier: The Quarter When Capacity Became The Question

## Core Question

What made 2026-Q2 feel less like a collection of separate crises and more like a test of institutional capacity?

## Provisional Thesis

The quarter's structural issue is not acceleration alone, but the widening gap between the speed of technological, geopolitical, demographic, and ecological pressure and the capacity of institutions to absorb, legitimate, and govern that pressure.

## Strongest Opposing Thesis

The quarter should not be over-unified. AI, war, democracy, displacement, and Korea's domestic questions may share timing but not a single institutional logic.
