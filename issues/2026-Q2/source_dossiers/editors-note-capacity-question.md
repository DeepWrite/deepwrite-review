# Source Dossier: The Quarter When Capacity Became The Question

## Independent Reporting Assignment

This article is assigned to the Final Managing Editor with Source Researcher. It must proceed as a magazine article, not a compact briefing skeleton. The public article should carry argument, evidence, cases, and prose; development notes belong here or in review files.

## Central Thesis

The issue's organizing frame is institutional capacity: whether public systems can translate acceleration, conflict, displacement, technological diffusion, democratic strain, and Korea's domestic pressures into legitimate action before pressure becomes distrust.
