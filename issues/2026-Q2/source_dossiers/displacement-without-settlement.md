# Source Dossier: Displacement Without Settlement

## Independent Reporting Assignment

This article is assigned to the Foreign Affairs Editor with Data Researcher. It must proceed as a magazine article, not a compact briefing skeleton. The public article should carry argument, evidence, cases, and prose; development notes belong here or in review files.

## Central Thesis

The structural problem in forced displacement is not only flight but the conversion of emergency into long-term civic suspension.
