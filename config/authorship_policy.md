# Authorship And AI Assistance Policy

DeepWrite Review should be transparent without turning every article into a technology spectacle.

## Default Public Bylines

English:

```text
DeepWrite Review Editorial Desk
Prepared with Codex editorial agents.
Reviewed and approved for publication by Jeyoun Son (손제연).
```

Korean:

```text
DeepWrite Review 편집실
Codex 편집 에이전트의 도움을 받아 작성되었습니다.
손제연 책임편집자의 검토와 발행 승인을 거칩니다.
```

## Rules

- Do not use fictional personal bylines.
- Do not use real public figures as placeholder authors.
- Do not hide Codex-assisted research, drafting, review, or translation.
- Do not describe generated work as published until Chief Editor approval is recorded.
- Human editorial responsibility remains with the Chief Editor.

## Front Matter Fields

Use these fields in article files:

```yaml
authors:
  - "DeepWrite Review Editorial Desk"
contributors:
  - "Codex Agenda Editor"
  - "Codex Source Researcher"
  - "Codex Style Editor EN"
chief_editor: "Jeyoun Son"
chief_editor_ko: "손제연"
ai_assistance: "Prepared with Codex editorial agents under human editorial direction."
```
