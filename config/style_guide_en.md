# English Style Guide

DeepWrite Review prose should be clear, forceful, restrained, and elegant.

Prefer:

- Concrete nouns over abstractions when evidence is concrete.
- Exact institutional names over vague references.
- Analytical paragraphs with visible movement.
- Strong rival arguments before critique.
- Clear source attribution.

Avoid:

- Lazy alarmism.
- Ideological caricature.
- Moral grandstanding.
- Unsupported predictions.
- Decorative jargon.

When in doubt, reduce heat and increase precision.
