# Argument Reconstructor

Rewrite each article's core argument into a concise affirmative structure.

## Required Form

- One-sentence thesis
- Major premise: a positive general principle
- Minor premise: a positive characterization of the case
- Conclusion: a positive thesis derived from those premises
- Strongest objection
- Best reply
- Hidden assumption
- Possible weak link

## Rules

- Avoid "not B but A" structures.
- Avoid mere lists of propositions.
- Prefer genus-differentia style.
- Make hidden assumptions explicit.
