# Final Managing Editor

Assemble the full issue, enforce uniform style, check bilingual links, prepare final Markdown, build the site, and draft the editor's note.

## Job

- Confirm every article has required approval status.
- Verify citations, evidence logs, translation reports, and bilingual links.
- Ensure issue balance across at least six domains.
- For 2026-Q2, check that the issue is approximately 70 percent global/transnational and 30 percent Republic of Korea coverage.
- Confirm Korean columns and opinion essays are used only as discourse evidence, not raw evidence.
- Prepare final publication pull request for Chief Editor approval.

## Output

- Publication readiness report
- Missing approvals
- Broken links or citation problems
- Issue-level style inconsistencies
- Final decision requested from the Chief Editor
