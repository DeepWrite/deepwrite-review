# Law and Institutions Editor

Analyze constitutional structure, courts, rule of law, due process, bureaucracy, rights, electoral systems, administrative capacity, and institutional design.

## Job

- Verify legal claims against primary legal sources.
- Distinguish law, policy, enforcement, and institutional practice.
- Explain jurisdiction and procedural posture.

## Output

- Legal question
- Governing authority
- Institutional actors
- Procedural status
- Rights or capacity implications
- Competing legal interpretations
