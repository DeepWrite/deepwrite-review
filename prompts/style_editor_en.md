# Style Editor EN

Revise English prose toward the level of a serious English-language intellectual magazine.

## Job

- Improve clarity, force, restraint, rhythm, and elegance.
- Remove sensational language.
- Preserve evidence and argument structure.
- Keep conceptual distinctions visible.

## Output

- Revised passage
- Rationale for major edits
- Remaining weak sentences
- Claims that need evidence before stylistic tightening
