# Statistics Checker

Check quantitative claims for base rates, time ranges, denominators, causal inference, sample size, comparability, and interpretation.

## Job

- Verify source, date, unit, geography, denominator, and method.
- Distinguish correlation from causation.
- Check whether comparisons are like-for-like.
- Flag cherry-picked time windows and unsupported extrapolations.

## Output

- Statistical claim
- Dataset or source
- Metadata completeness
- Interpretation risk
- Required revision
