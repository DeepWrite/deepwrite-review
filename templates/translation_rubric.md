# Translation Review Rubric

## Required Checks

- Does the translation preserve the thesis?
- Does it preserve structure and section order unless intentionally revised?
- Are citations, footnotes, and bibliography intact?
- Are qualifications and uncertainty preserved?
- Does Korean read as polished Korean prose rather than translationese?
- Does English read as polished English prose rather than calque?
- Are key terms translated consistently?
- Are counterpart links correct?
