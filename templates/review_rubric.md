# Article Review Rubric

## Required Checks

- Does the article begin from an actual contemporary issue or structural change?
- Are contested factual claims sourced?
- Are statistical claims complete with source, date, unit, geography, denominator, and limitations?
- Does the article distinguish fact, interpretation, hypothesis, normative judgment, and speculation?
- Is the strongest opposing view represented fairly?
- Are ideological rivals reconstructed rather than caricatured?
- Are evidence strength and uncertainty explicit?
- Are citations real and traceable?
- Is the article approved for the current editorial stage?

## Recommendation

approve / revise / reject / hold
